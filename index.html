Hello, I am Vinita!

I am an aspiring junior web designer with creative ambitions. This portfolio contains websites for fictitious companies and highlights my skills in web design and development. All of these use HTML and CSS.

Make Content


In this project, I attempted to create a website for a media company. This is a fake company which “recruits” all kinds of media creators and stars to one another to produce entertainment content. The goal was to make the site vibrant enough for users. I took inspiration from sites with a similar theme like Spotify. I made a homepage, an “About” page, a “Posts” page, and a “Contact” page. While making this project, I learned how to create detailed input forms.

Ladies' Place


This is my attempt at creating a shopping website. The goal was to give the site a very classy appearance as the theme was a fashion site. I made mockup designs for a product and then placed those variations of a product in a catalog page. I also made a homepage to advertise the product, and a “Statement” page to talk about the brand. I learned how to create aspect ratios for divs, properly fit images, and use figcaptions.

Calm Beans


This is a "coming soon" landing page for a coffee shop. The goal of the site was to give off a relaxed feeling that one would get when they visit a coffee shop. Hence, I used a lot of brown and light, neutral colors on the page. I created three divs for the background colors and images and three divs for the form inputs and text.
